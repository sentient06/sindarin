Swadesh list

A Swadesh list is a compilation of forms and concepts which all languages, without exception, have terms for, such as star, hand, water, kill, sleep, and so forth. The number of such terms is small â€“ a few hundred at most, or possibly less than a hundred. The inclusion or exclusion of many terms is up to debate and there are several different lists. The Swadesh list is named after linguist Morris Swadesh. Read more about it on Wikipedia.
