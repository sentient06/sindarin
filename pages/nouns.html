Summary

Nouns in Sindarin are inflected into plural through vowel mutations. There is also a class plural and a fossilised dual form.

The mutation that causes most plurals is known as "i-affection".

Most diphthongs are immune to i-affection except in monosyllables. 

  Singular:
  galadh    = "tree"

  Plural:
  gelaidh   = "trees"

  Class plural:
  galadhath = "all trees"

  Dual remnant:
  galadhad  = "two trees"
