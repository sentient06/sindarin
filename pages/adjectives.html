Summary

Adjectives are inflected into the plural and must match their noun. They appear after the noun and are lenited.

Predicative statements and attributive statements can be identical. Reversing the position of the adjective can help disambiguate. 

  edhel dond = "[an] elf [is] tall" or "[a] tall elf"
  tond edhel = "tall [is] [an] elf"
